The adventure takes place in the kingdom of Valeria. The capital is Thornhold, a walled city ruled by Queen Maren the Unbroken.
The currency is gold coins. Magic is rare and feared by common folk.
The Whispering Woods east of Thornhold hide goblin camps and older, darker things.
The Ember Peaks to the north are home to dragons; nobody who climbed past the tree line has returned.
The Gilded Flagon inn in Thornhold is where every adventure begins.
